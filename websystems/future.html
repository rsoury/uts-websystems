Where do I see myself in 5 years?

After I finish university, I hope to utilise all the skills I have accumulated to work at a large software business, that primarily deals in consumer internet where I can have some influence on their product.

I have a strong interest in possibly joining an early stage startup or at least working my way to join an early stage startup. I want to see something scalable grow before my very eyes and be in an environment where every moment counts.

Technology is changing so rapidly, these targets will surely change and adapt to the new market, and new prospects.

In regards to short term futuristic interests, I have considered really delving into artificial intelligence as it seems to be the way in which software is moving. Being capable of converting experience into data. I have briefly read some of Google Tensor Flow's Documentation just to dip my toes in the really complicated and intimidating water and am left a little bit curious. I think it is going to require years on research and study to understand which could be a knowledge based adventure for another time.
